Some general instructions to create an assessment:

  1. Go to "Create Assessment" page in dashboard and create an assessment id.
  2. After creating assessment id, go to "Upload Question Paper" page for extracting questions and saving.
  3. After saving questions. Add the photos of attendees who will be taking the examination.
  4. Finally your exam will be created successfully and you can verify your assessment details by opening "Your Assessments" page.

Questions structure to be followed in PDF:(‚ö†Ô∏èIf not in the below format the questions cannot be extracted properly.)

  • For MCQ Questions ensure the question is followed by either of the options form:
    • A.

  • For fill in the blank question, the question should end with either . or ? and should contain atleast 3 underscores "___" anywhere in the question.
  • For Textual question the question should end with either . or ?
  • For MCQ and Fill in the Blank the correct answer should be mentioned in next line after questions in the following format:
    • Answer: your_answer
